About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of final-year students from the University of Wollongong who are passionate about solving real-world problems through innovation. Our carpooling platform, UniRide, was developed as a sustainable solution to the challenges of daily commuting, with a focus on connecting university students and staff.

By facilitating shared rides, UniRide helps reduce traffic congestion and cut down transportation costs, all while fostering a more eco-conscious community.

Our Mission

Greener Commutes, Stronger Connections

Our mission is to pave the way for a greener future by revolutionizing the way students and staff commute. We aim to minimize our environmental footprint by promoting carpooling as an accessible, eco-friendly alternative to traditional transportation methods.

Through UniRide, we strive to make sustainable commuting not only practical but also enjoyable, building connections within our university community while championing environmental responsibility.

Environmental Impact

Join us in making a difference


We believe that small actions can lead to significant change. Through UniRide, we aim to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and minimize the environmental footprint of our daily commutes. Every shared ride contributes to cleaner air, fewer vehicles on the road, and a greener future.

Cost Savings

Save while you ride


With UniRide, you not only contribute to a sustainable environment but also enjoy financial benefits. Carpooling allows drivers to share costs, reducing fuel expenses and making commuting more budget-friendly. Riders also benefit from affordable rides, ensuring that everyone saves money on their journey.

Affordability and Accessibility

Connecting communities through affordable transportation


UniRide is designed to bring drivers and riders together, offering a cost-effective commuting solution for university students and staff. We strive to make transportation accessible to all, providing an easy-to-use platform that ensures everyone can travel comfortably and affordably.

Frequently Asked Questions

UniRide is a carpooling service designed for university students and staff to reduce costs and help the environment.

Becoming a UniRide rider/driver is easy. Register with your student/staff credentials and submit your vehicle details(drivers only) for approval.

Carpooling with UniRide helps you save money, reduce your carbon footprint, and make new friends along the way!

UniRide offers customization & personalization such as Cross-Border carpooling and Women-only rides. It is built specifically for students and staff, offering an eco-friendly and cost-effective solution with a strong community focus.

Currently, UniRide is designed exclusively for students and staff, but we are exploring the possibility of expanding to the broader community.

UniRide is free for students and staff. Ride costs are determined by the drivers and it is definitely cheaper than other alternatives.

UniRide verifies students and staff credentials. We also provide driver and rider reviews to ensure a safe and trusted community.

UniRide maintains high service standards for its users and failing to meet these standards could lead to a temporary suspension on the user's account.

You can contact UniRide by clicking on 'Contact Us' on the navigation bar. We will respond within 2 working days.

We are sorry to hear that you have lost your item and understand you maybe distressed about this. You can contact your driver directly if your trip ended not less than 24 hours ago, else you can call us at +65 6655 5566.
